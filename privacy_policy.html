3. Data We Collect

This website collects minimal data. It does not use forms, user accounts, analytics, or tracking of any kind.

3.1 localStorage Data
This game uses your browser's localStorage to store the following preferences locally on your device:

• Mute preference — whether sound is muted or unmuted
• Framerate setting — your chosen framerate (30–360 FPS)

This data is stored exclusively in your browser. It is never transmitted to any server, never leaves your device, and contains no personal information. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

3.2 Technical Data (Hosting)
When you visit this website, your browser automatically transmits certain technical data to the hosting provider (e.g., GitHub Pages). This may include:

• IP address
• Browser type and version
• Operating system
• Date and time of access
• Referring URL

This data is processed by the hosting provider and is not accessible to us.

4. Purposes and Legal Basis

• localStorage (game preferences) — Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in providing a functional game experience. No personal data is processed.
• Website hosting & security — Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — legitimate interest in operating a secure and accessible website.

5. Third-Party Processors

This website does not use any third-party services for analytics, tracking, fonts, or content delivery. All assets (fonts, images, sounds) are served locally.

The only third party involved is the hosting provider (e.g., GitHub Pages), which processes technical access data (IP address, request logs) as part of standard web hosting. This processing is governed by the hosting provider's own privacy policy.

6. International Data Transfers

If this website is hosted on GitHub Pages, technical access data may be processed in the United States by GitHub, Inc. GitHub is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (DPF), which provides an adequate level of data protection pursuant to the European Commission's adequacy decision of 10 July 2023.

No other international data transfers occur.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website does not use cookies. No cookie consent banner is required or displayed.

We use your browser's localStorage to store game preferences (mute state and framerate) locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to any server.

8. Retention Periods

• localStorage data — Until you clear your browser data.
• Server/access logs (hosting provider) — Per hosting provider policy (typically 30 days).

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

• Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR) — You may request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and, if so, obtain a copy of that data.
• Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) — You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
• Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) — You may request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
• Right to restriction (Art. 18 GDPR) — You may request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
• Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR) — You may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
• Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR) — You may object to processing based on legitimate interests at any time, for reasons related to your particular situation.
• Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR) — Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at simon@heistermann-solutions.de. We will respond within one month of receiving your request.
